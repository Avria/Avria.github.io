About Us

We are a club in Mason High School called the "Animal Shelter Alliance." Our objective is to improve the welfare and future of animals in shelters across the Cincinnati region. We will raise awareness for the humane society and advocate care for homeless animals. We will help out multiple shelters through teamwork and volunteering, including making toys and treats for homeless pets.
